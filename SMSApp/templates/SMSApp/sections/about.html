What is it?

SMS app is here to help remind you!

More specifically, its a tool that allows the creation of instant one-time or scheduled reminders. All of which are delivered directly to your phone at the time you specify.

How does it work?

It is as simple as 3 steps!

Step 1: Enter your phone number or a friends phone number that you need to remind.

Step 2: Select a date or time to be reminded.

Step 3: Enter your reminder!

Click the button and you're done!
